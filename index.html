Frequently asked questions

A few of the questions people ask us most often. If yours is not here, get in touch and we will be glad to help.

What areas do you cover?
We act for clients across North East Fife and the wider region, from our offices in St Andrews, Cupar, Glenrothes and Auchtermuchty. Our head office is at 6 Bell Street in St Andrews, and we are happy to help you wherever you are based in Fife.
What legal services do you offer?
We are a full-service practice. We advise on family and matrimonial law, wills, powers of attorney and executries, residential and commercial property, and court work including civil disputes and employment matters. Whatever your situation, you will work with a named solicitor who knows your case.
How do I make an appointment?
You can call our St Andrews office on 01334 472188, or complete the enquiry form on this page and the right solicitor will be in touch. We see clients by appointment, Monday to Friday.
Are you regulated?
Yes. Rollos Law LLP is regulated by the Law Society of Scotland, and our solicitors are subject to its professional standards and conduct rules. You can instruct us with confidence that your matter is handled to those standards.
Do you handle both personal and business legal matters?
We do. We act for individuals and families on matters such as family law, wills and home moves, and for businesses and landlords on commercial property, leases and employment matters. Many of our clients come to us for both their personal and their business affairs.
Why choose an independent Fife firm?
As an independent firm rooted in North East Fife, we give you direct access to the solicitor handling your matter and advice from people who know the local area. You are not passed between departments or treated as a file number.
Will I deal with the same solicitor throughout?
In most cases, yes. We believe your matter should be overseen by a named solicitor who is accountable to you from your first call through to the conclusion of your case, so you always know who to speak to.
